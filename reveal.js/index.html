## Three Laws of TDD
Thou shall not write production code without a failing test.
Thou shall not write more unit tests than required to fail.
Thou shall not write more production code than required to pass a failing test.

Let's Bowl!!

Implement rules of bowling scoring with TDD

Points for each frame are the number of pins knocked down with max two rolls
tallied after a frame
unless...
10 pins knocked down in two rolls
e.g. 2,8, but not 10,0
That's a spare
Spare frame counts as 10 + next roll pins
tallied after the that roll
10 pins knocked down in a single roll
That's a strike
Spare frame counts as 10 + next two roll pins
tallied after the those rolls

Bonus rounds

Validation
There are exactly 10 frames per game
You can knocked down at max 10 pins during a frame
except the last frame...
for strike on first roll of the last frame you get two additional rolls
for spare on first two rolls of the last frame you get one additional roll
